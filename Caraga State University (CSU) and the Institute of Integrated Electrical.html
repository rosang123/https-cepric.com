July 9, 2024

Caraga State University (CSU) and the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc. (IIEE) officially signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) on August 9, 2024, during the 26th Northern Mindanao Regional Conference at Almont Inland Resort, Butuan City. This MOU sets the stage for collaborative efforts in research, education, and extension activities, aiming to enhance immersion programs, improve education quality through internships, and drive joint research projects and events. Key attendees included IIEE National President Dr. Florigo C. Varona, IIEE Northern Mindanao Regional Governor Tomas Virtudez, IIEE-Butuan-Agusan del Norte Chapter President Arnel B. Alipao, CSU President Rolyn C. Daguil, CSUCC Campus Director Dr. Deomila B. Mamaoag, CEIT Dean Engr. Blondie T. Sanchez, CEPRIC Center Chief Engr. Erickson A. Tacda, and other distinguished leaders from both institutions..
