About the CSUCC Energy and Power Research and Innovation Center

The CSUCC Energy and Power Research & Innovation Center (CEPRIC) is dedicated to advancing energy and power technologies. Located at Caraga State University Cabadbaran Campus, we aim to lead in innovation, research, and development in energy systems and power generation.

Vision

To be a leading research and innovation center recognized for excellence in energy and power research, contributing to the advancement of energy technologies and sustainable practices..

Mission

Our mission is to spearhead research and innovation in energy and power systems, supporting sustainable development and technological progress. We strive to provide cutting-edge solutions to global energy challenges and contribute to local and national energy sectors.

Objectives

  • Conduct high-impact research in energy and power technologies.
  • Promote sustainable energy practices and innovations.
  • Collaborate with industry partners and academic institutions.
  • Provide training and development opportunities for students and professionals.
  • Support the development and implementation of cutting-edge energy solutions.
